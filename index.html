About MPCRC

Mannat physio care and rehab center has the best physiotherapist team. All the physiotherapist are well trained and skilled to treat the patient. Our therapist are best to diagnose accurately all the conditions. We give the treatment in orthopaedic as well as neurological conditions. We also provide a quality rehabilitation in sports injuries. Our aim to provide the best treatment to the patients as to decrease their duration of treatment and also their pain. We provide treatment in every perspective so that patient can do their activities of daily living. We also provide home visit to the patient. In home visits we use the modalities also. As health comes first, so we aim to give a better health.


MPCRC AIM

My main aim is to prevent further problems associated with the conditions and to promote a healthy lifestyle. And also to find out the cause of injury to prevent reoccurrence of the problem. For me diagnosis is the foremost thing in the treatment.

A correct diagnosis prevents further chances of injuries. My treatment protocol cure my patients pain and gives them a active lifestyle.
